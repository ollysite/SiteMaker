Inspiring Change: Patient Blood Management in Asia-Pacific

The Asia-Pacific region is a vibrant and diverse part of the world, home to over half of the world's population. It is also a region where significant health challenges, such as anemia, are prevalent, affecting an estimated 30-50% of the population.

This inspiration board is a call to action. It highlights the challenges and showcases the innovative work being done in Patient Blood Management (PBM) and blood health throughout this vast region to improve patient outcomes.
